About Us

Frescura

A little story about us

FresCura India Pvt. Ltd. is an organization working on making the post-harvest supply chain market a friendly ecosystem for consumers and farmers. We aim to provide end to end farm to user solution for fruits and vegetables on Business to customer and Business to Business platforms. Being in gated communities ourselves, our society meeting’s main issues revolved around getting fresh produce, be it vegetables or fruits, so we decided to come up with an answer. We collaborated with high end apartments, townships and gated communities & villas and provide them with a comprehensive and convenient shopping experience at the doorstep of the residents. We started our own Walk-in Mobile supermarket on a trucks with state of the art modifications which is designed to deliver the freshest produce. Our consumers have the opportunity to hand pick from the best produce themselves


Company Vision

Our motto being “Producer’s prosperity, Consumer delight”, we are focusing towards creating a niche for ourselves in the retail space by creating the world’s first temperature controlled walk in mobile superstore.


Why collaborate with us

  1. A convenient and reliable option for the purchase of fruits and vegetables for all the residents at their doorstep.
  2. Produce is picked up directly from farmers and hence there is better control of product safety and quality.
  3. Offer a wide range of products including organic, exotic and local vegetables and fruits.
  4. Products priced competitively compared to the existing retail stores and weekly markets.
