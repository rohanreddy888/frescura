FresCura India Pvt. Ltd. is an organization working on making the post-harvest supply chain market a friendly ecosystem for users and farmers. We aim to provide end to end farm to user solution for fruits and vegetables on Business to customer and Business to Business platforms.

Services

  1. . A firsthand experience to choose and purchase freshly picked fruits and vegetables kept in a temperature controlled container at the doorstep of customers, creating the world’s first farm fresh walk in mobile superstore.

  3. . Daily deliveries throughout hyderabad to Restaurants / Retailers.
  4. . Our Exclusive Arrangements with Gated Communities/villas thro our mobile superstore has put us on the map and heart of hyderabad.
